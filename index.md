I am currently a Master's student in the School of Instrument Science and Engineering at Southeast University, advised by Prof. Aiguo Song. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree from Southeast University in 2024 and spent time as a Visiting Student at the Advanced Robotics Lab at Great Bay University, working with Prof. Michael Yu Wang. My research interests lie at the intersection of **robotic perception, vision-tactile fusion, and dexterous manipulation**.
